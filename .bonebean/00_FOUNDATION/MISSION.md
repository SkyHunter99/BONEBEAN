# Mission

## Purpose

This document defines how Bone Bean AI achieves its long-term vision.

---

# Mission Statement

Build the most reliable, extensible, and intelligent platform for creating and operating deterministic trading systems.

---

# Core Missions

## Mission 1

Provide institutional-grade infrastructure for automated trading.

---

## Mission 2

Separate deterministic trading logic from probabilistic AI reasoning.

---

## Mission 3

Enable strategy creation without requiring users to build infrastructure.

---

## Mission 4

Create an extensible plugin ecosystem.

---

## Mission 5

Continuously improve strategy quality using AI-assisted analysis while keeping execution deterministic.

---

## Mission 6

Deliver a secure multi-tenant SaaS platform suitable for individual traders and professional teams.

---

# Engineering Mission

Every component should be:

- Reusable
- Testable
- Observable
- Documented
- Versioned

---

# User Mission

Allow users to focus on strategy rather than infrastructure.

---

# Success Indicators

Mission success is measured by:

- Strategy reliability
- Platform stability
- User productivity
- Plugin ecosystem growth
- Marketplace adoption
- Customer retention
