# Vision

## Purpose

This document defines the long-term vision of Bone Bean AI.

The vision serves as the north star for every architectural decision, engineering investment, and business strategy. All future development should align with this vision.

---

# Vision Statement

Bone Bean AI aims to become the leading AI-powered operating system for liquidity providers, quantitative traders, and digital asset managers.

The platform enables users to design, validate, optimize, deploy, monitor, and evolve deterministic trading strategies through an extensible, secure, and intelligent ecosystem.

---

# Long-Term Vision

Bone Bean AI is designed to become more than a trading application.

It will evolve into a complete engineering platform for:

- Strategy Development
- Strategy Automation
- Strategy Marketplace
- AI-Assisted Strategy Design
- Portfolio Intelligence
- Risk Management
- Research & Backtesting
- Multi-Provider Integration
- Multi-Chain Expansion

---

# Product Vision

Provide a unified platform where every trading strategy can be:

- Designed
- Simulated
- Validated
- Deployed
- Monitored
- Improved

without requiring users to rebuild infrastructure.

---

# Engineering Vision

The platform should remain:

- Modular
- Deterministic
- Observable
- Secure
- Extensible
- AI Assisted
- Plugin Driven

---

# Business Vision

Bone Bean AI will build an ecosystem where:

- Users create strategies.
- AI improves strategies.
- Developers build plugins.
- Experts publish reusable strategies.
- Customers consume professional-grade automation.

---

# Success Criteria

Bone Bean AI becomes the trusted operating platform for professional digital asset strategy management.
