# Operations

The Operations domain defines how Bone Bean AI is deployed, monitored, maintained, and operated in production.

Its objective is to ensure the platform remains reliable, observable, scalable, recoverable, and secure throughout its operational lifecycle.

# Operational Principles

- Automate repetitive operations.
- Everything is observable.
- Monitor before failure.
- Infrastructure should be reproducible.
- Every incident must produce a postmortem.
- Every production change must be traceable.
- Recovery procedures must be documented and tested.
