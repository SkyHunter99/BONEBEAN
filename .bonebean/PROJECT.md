# Bone Bean AI

## Project

Bone Bean AI is an AI-powered Liquidity Provider Operating System built for Meteora DLMM on Solana.

Bone Bean AI is not a trading bot.

It is a complete operating system that enables users to build, test, optimize, automate and monetize Liquidity Provider strategies.

---

## Mission

Build the world's best Liquidity Provider Operating System.

The platform must enable anyone to:

• Discover opportunities
• Build strategies visually
• Import strategies using AI
• Backtest strategies
• Paper trade strategies
• Automate strategies
• Sell strategies
• Learn from strategies

## Revenue Model

Revenue comes from Strategy Marketplace.

Creators publish strategies.

Users purchase strategies.

Bone Bean AI receives marketplace commission.

Users bring their own AI API keys.

Market APIs are managed by Bone Bean AI.
